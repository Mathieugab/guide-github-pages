What do you have in the guide?

The guide welcomes, informs, and help your Airbnb guests.

Welcome message

Welcome your guests with your own words. They will appreciate especially if you cannot welcome them personnaly.

WIFI and internet connexion

Give your internet access. This is usually the first thing guest are asking and looking for when they just enter your flat.

Directions

Tell them what's the easiest way to go to your place. You can give the best options to go to your place.

Check-in process

You can give back the check-in process (codes, hours for the check-in, ...)

Help with equipments

How does the TV work ? Where can i find the ironer ?You always have the same questions from your guests, answer all of them in your BNB GUIDE

Activities around

You can show what are the activities in the neighborhood. And share your best places to visit!

Your favorite restaurants

Give recommandations to your guests on the best restaurants around.
